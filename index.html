WeSwim Swimming Team

Wenhao Fang, a 10th-grade student at Canyon Crest Academy, has been passionate about swimming since he started at the age of 6. He has competed with swimming clubs in both Houston and San Diego and is currently a member of the J-Barracuda team in San Diego, where he continues to swim competitively. Wenhao, who excels in freestyle and butterfly, was a championship swimmer during his time at Penn Oak Middle School. Now, he is eager to share his expertise with younger children, helping them master the four main strokes: freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, and butterfly. He believes that proper technique is the key to becoming a stronger swimmer and enjoys breaking down each stroke into manageable steps, allowing kids to build confidence and improve their skills in the water.

WeSwim Swimming Team

I am Wenhao Fang, a 10th-grade student at Canyon Crest Academy. I have been passionate about swimming since I started at the age of 6. I have competed with swimming clubs in both Houston and San Diego and is currently a member of the J-Barracuda team in San Diego, where I continues to swim competitively. I, who excels in freestyle and butterfly, was a championship swimmer during my time at Pin Oak Middle School in Houston. This summer (2024), I and my sister Emily Fang founded the WeSwim swimming team, with the name derived from the first letters of our names. I am eager to share my expertise with younger children, helping them master the four main strokes: freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, and butterfly. I believe that proper technique is the key to becoming a stronger swimmer and enjoys breaking down each stroke into manageable steps, allowing kids to build confidence and improve their skills in the water.
